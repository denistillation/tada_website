Getting to Berkeley

Berkeley is located in the East Bay, across the bay from San Francisco. The campus is accessible by BART, bus, and car.

  • BART: Take BART to Downtown Berkeley station, a short walk from campus.
  • Airports: Oakland (OAK) is the closest airport; San Francisco (SFO) is also convenient via BART.
  • Parking: Limited visitor parking is available on and near campus. Details will be posted before the event.

Accommodation

Berkeley and the surrounding East Bay offer a range of hotels and short-term rentals. We recommend booking early, especially if you plan to stay in Berkeley itself. A list of suggested accommodations will be added here.
