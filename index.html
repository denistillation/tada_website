About the Conference

Computational approaches to text

Text as Data convenes scholars using computational and statistical methods to study language, discourse, and textual sources. Join us for presentations, discussion, and community-building at UC Berkeley.

Presentations

Talks and posters on methods, applications, and theory in text-as-data research.

Community

Connect with researchers across disciplines who share an interest in textual data.

Berkeley

Hosted on the UC Berkeley campus in the heart of the Bay Area.

Get Involved

We welcome your work

We invite submissions from graduate students, postdocs, faculty, and researchers in industry. Presentations may cover new methods, empirical applications, reproducibility, or open questions in the field.
